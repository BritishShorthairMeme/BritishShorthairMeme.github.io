Toilet Protocol

Toilet Protocol is a cryptocurrency that aims to recycle the trash tokens that exist on the Polygon Mainnet network. Too many tokens that are no longer useful on the Blockchain such as fraudulent tokens, rugpull, tokens that cannot be traded on the market and so on.

The mechanism of this protocol is that the trash token that is owned is sent to the Toilet Protocol smart contract so that this smart contract will issue a new token that is pTOI or can be called Proof of Recycle (PoR).

How is the pTOI token different from other tokens?

Many Coins and Tokens out there use Proof of Work (PoW) and Proof of Stake (PoS). Toilet Protocol uses an unprecedented Proof of Recycle (PoR), new tokens that can be printed through recycling.

What is the total supply of pTOI tokens?

pTOI tokens will only be issued as much as 2.625.000 so having it is very valuable and rare. The total supply of pTOI is 8 times less than the total supply of BTC.

Why Protocol Toilets use the Polygon Mainnet network?

Because the transaction fees carried out do not require very expensive fees and also have speed in transacting on the Blockchain. Unlike Bitcoin, Ethereum and Binance coins, the Polygon Mainnet network is an alternative for transactions, thus helping people in their daily activities.
